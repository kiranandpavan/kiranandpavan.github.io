Our Story

Our story began in February 2017. We met each other through our parents and initially neither was convinced with the idea of an arranged marriage. We decided to give it a shot anyway and we sure are glad to have done so.

The first conversation was definitely a bit awkward for the both of us. A couple of calls later things started to feel a bit more natural and our conversations became more interesting. Fortunately, these feelings were mutual. Days passed by and we were talking and texting all the time we can grab. In a couple of months, we felt strongly about our relationship right around the first time we met in May.

Then at start of July, we planned a short trip to Bangalore for the graduation ceremony. This panned out perfectly as we now had more time to ourselves and could get to know each other even better. There was a moment we discovered ourselves and that’s the point we felt lucky to have found each other and realised that we were meant to be together.

Things were moving so fast and everything started falling into place. We got engaged on 23rd of July at a beautiful ceremony and we are getting hitched on October 6th, 2017. Cannot wait any longer to spend our lives together and adding more pages to our story.
